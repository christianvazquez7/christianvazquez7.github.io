Trailzz: A Crowdsourced decision-making engine
Because we all hate making decisions, but love telling others what to do.
Project Description
Trailzz is a startup venture to develop a mobile application that engages and helps users in their everyday decision-making process. Trailzz alleviates the decision-making stress faced by people in their daily lives by transforming decision-making into a collaborative process that taps into the collective wisdom of the crowds. Trailzz is not a social network, it is an engine that promotes the "game of influence", where every decision you help make has a direct reaction in the real world.
