VRDrums
Project Description
A playful exploration of musical expression in Virtual Reality. Using the HTC Vive and a handful of Vive trackers, we can convey the nuances of live musical performances in virtual environments. Players can embody avatars and play the drums as they do in the real world. Performances can happen in unexpected locations and impossible scenarios!
