Keeping in touch with [TRIBE]
Project Description
Tribe is an Android application that explores how stimulating conversations with a generated topic can help you stay connected with friends and family. It is common for long distance acquaintances to slowly drift apart, despite our inherent desire to keep in touch. Tribe is a simple solution to this problem. You input the phone numbers of people who want to be a part of your [Tribe]. Sporadically, our app will generate an interesting topic and text on of your friend from your phone number. This can help spark a conversation, and foster interactions with those who are far from you. Keep in touch with your tribe!
